<div style={{ maxWidth: 760 }}>

<h2 style={{ fontSize: "22px", marginBottom: 8 }}>Overview</h2>

<p style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>
  The <strong>AI Asset Recommendation</strong> API provides a data-driven ranking and evaluation of crypto assets
  listed in the <strong>Bithumb KRW</strong> market. It combines market data with multi-dimensional scoring and
  AI-generated interpretation.
</p>

<Note>
  This endpoint is labeled <strong>Mock</strong> in the spec. Treat outputs as informational snapshots.
</Note>

<h3 style={{ marginTop: 20 }}>Two core components</h3>

<ul style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>
  <li>
    <strong>Recommendation List</strong>: ranked assets based on a composite <code>Total Score</code> (0–10),
    computed as the rounded average of five category scores.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Recommendation Details</strong>: in-depth evaluation for each asset, including score interpretation,
    category breakdown, price context, and social sentiment distribution.
  </li>
</ul>

<h3 style={{ marginTop: 20 }}>Scoring model</h3>

<div style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>
  <p style={{ marginBottom: 8 }}>
    Total Score is derived from five categories (each 0–10):
  </p>

  <ul>
    <li><strong>Market Sentiment</strong></li>
    <li><strong>Price Stability</strong></li>
    <li><strong>Search Trend</strong></li>
    <li><strong>Trading Activity</strong></li>
    <li><strong>Upside Energy</strong></li>
  </ul>

  <p style={{ marginTop: 10 }}>
    The response includes both <code>score_by_category</code> and <code>score_interpretation</code>.
  </p>
</div>

<h3 style={{ marginTop: 20 }}>How to use</h3>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provide asset candidates">
    Send an array of assets with <code>ticker</code> and <code>current</code> price (required per item). You may include
    <code>change_rate</code> when available.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optional: attach timestamp">
    Include <code>timestamp</code> to annotate the snapshot timing (if your pipeline tracks it).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read ranking + evaluation">
    Use <code>ranking.overall_rank</code> and <code>score.total</code> to sort, then display <code>ai_evaluation.summary</code> /
    <code>ai_evaluation.opinion</code> for the narrative layer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If certain data is unavailable or not applicable, corresponding fields may be <code>null</code> or omitted.
</Warning>

</div>
