<div style={{ maxWidth: 760 }}>

<h2 style={{ fontSize: "22px", marginBottom: 8 }}>Overview</h2>

<p style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>
  The <strong>Key Trends</strong> API provides trend summaries that may impact the crypto market, with emphasis on
  macro and market-specific factors relevant to the KR market.
</p>

<Note>
  This endpoint returns a <strong>point-in-time snapshot</strong>. If <code>date</code> is omitted, the latest available
  snapshot is returned.
</Note>

<h3 style={{ marginTop: 20 }}>How to use</h3>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Optional: choose a date">
    Provide <code>date</code> (YYYY-MM-DD). If omitted, the latest available snapshot is returned.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Selected Version">
    Choose from a total of 5 version to fit in your demand for daily market trends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Outputs are for informational purposes only and should not be interpreted as financial advice.
</Warning>

</div>
