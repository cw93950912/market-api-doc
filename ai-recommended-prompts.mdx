<h2 style={{ fontSize: "22px", marginBottom: 8 }}>Overview</h2>

<p style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>
  The <strong>AI Recommended Prompts</strong> API generates context-aware analytical questions and structured
  responses for the <strong>Bithumb KRW</strong> crypto market. It helps users quickly understand market conditions,
  asset movements, capital flows, and emerging narratives.
</p>

<Note>
  Prompts include both <strong>fixed core questions</strong> and <strong>dynamically selected questions</strong> from a pool
  that may change over time.
</Note>

<h3 style={{ marginTop: 20 }}>Prompt categories</h3>

<ul style={{ fontSize: 15, lineHeight: 1.8 }}>
  <li><strong>Market sentiment</strong>: overall mood & breadth</li>
  <li><strong>Momentum</strong>: strongest movers and why</li>
  <li><strong>Trending issues</strong>: what the market is talking about</li>
  <li><strong>Directional picks</strong>: <code>go_up</code> / <code>go_down</code> (may require <code>ticker</code>)</li>
  <li><strong>Narratives</strong>: KOL discussions / sector rotations</li>
</ul>

<h3 style={{ marginTop: 20 }}>How to use</h3>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a type">
    Set <code>type</code> (required) as the prompt template key. Different types produce different structured outputs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optional: choose a date">
    Provide <code>date</code> (YYYY-MM-DD) to view historical context. Default is the latest available snapshot.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optional: provide ticker (only for some types)">
    When <code>type</code> is <code>go_up</code> or <code>go_down</code>, you should pass <code>ticker</code>.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Outputs are for informational purposes only and should not be interpreted as financial advice.
</Warning>
